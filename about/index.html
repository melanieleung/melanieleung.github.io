About

Nice to meet you!

I am interested in both teaching and using spatial data science for the purposes of sustainable natural resource management. You can see what I am mostly up to on my LinkedIn I also occasionally tweet @melaniesleung" This year, I worked for NASA's DEVELOP program on aquatic remote sensing projects: the Florida Transportation & Infrastructure Project, and the Southern California Health & Air Quality Project Using satellite imagery, my team and I worked to create graphical user interfaces to help monitor water quality conditions such as turbidity and chlorophyll-a trends. Right now, I am working on a remote sensing project with a lab in UCLA's Geography department. I process drone images to monitor trends of kelp canopies in the northern coast of California. Before I got into geographic information systems (GIS), I did a decent amount of graphic design work. Sometimes I still mess around in Procreate and Adobe Illustrator. I'm trying to use old design skills to make cool maps now- I think cartography is pretty neat.
